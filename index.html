Our Story

Fragrance is
memory
made tangible.

KingTori Scents was born from a simple belief: that every person deserves to carry a signature that commands attention. We source the finest fragrances — from the souks of Dubai to the perfumeries of Paris — and bring them to your doorstep in Lagos. As the popular saying goes - Naija no dey carry last!

Our collection spans bold orientals, airy florals, summer fresh and deep woody accords. Whatever your story, we have the scent that tells it.
