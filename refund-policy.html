Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: June 2026  ·  KingTori Scents, Lagos, Nigeria

At KingTori Scents, your satisfaction is our priority. We take great care to ensure every order is packed correctly and delivered in perfect condition. If something is not right, we want to make it right — quickly and fairly.

Please read our policy carefully before requesting a return or refund.

⏱️

Return Window

48 Hours

All return requests must be made within 48 hours of receiving your order.

🔒

We only accept returns on items that are factory sealed and completely unopened.

✅

Wrong Item

01

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or refund, the following conditions must be met:

The return request must be submitted within 48 hours of receiving your order. Requests made after this window will not be accepted.

The item must be factory sealed and completely unopened. Any product that has been opened, used, or tampered with is not eligible for return regardless of the reason.

Returns are accepted in the following cases only: you received the wrong fragrance, or the product arrived damaged due to packaging or transit.

Change of mind, dislike of scent, or ordering the wrong product are not eligible for a refund. We encourage customers to reach out via WhatsApp before ordering if they have any questions about a fragrance.

02

How to Request a Return

Follow these steps to initiate a return:

1
Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order via WhatsApp at +234 706 344 5321 or email at kingtorigroup@gmail.com.
2
Provide your order details — your full name, Paystack payment reference, and a clear photo or video showing the issue (wrong item or damage).
3
Await confirmation — our team will review your request and respond within 24 hours with next steps.
4
Return the item — if approved, we will arrange pickup or provide a return address. The item must be in its original sealed packaging.
5
Refund or replacement — once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days, or we will dispatch the correct item immediately.

03

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 3–5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at checkout. If payment was made via bank transfer, the refund will be made to the same bank account.

KingTori Scents reserves the right to reject a return if the item does not meet the eligibility criteria stated above.

04

Return Shipping Costs

If you received the wrong item or a damaged product due to our error, KingTori Scents will cover the full cost of return shipping. We will arrange pickup directly from your delivery address.

For any other approved return scenario, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

05

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

— Opened or used fragrances, regardless of the amount used.

— Items returned after the 48-hour window has elapsed.

— Items purchased during a sale or promotional event, unless damaged or incorrect.

— Gift sets that have been partially opened or used.

Our team is available to help. Reach out via WhatsApp for the fastest response — we typically reply within the hour.
